DOOM on wasm-posix-kernel

unmodified fbDOOM running in your browser via the kernel's /dev/fb0 Linux fbdev surface.

Place a Doom IWAD at /assets/doom/doom1.wad (see README) then click Start.

Click the canvas to give it keyboard focus. The browser sends AT-set-1 scancodes (the Linux MEDIUMRAW protocol) over stdin; fbDOOM decodes them via its own scancode → DOOM-key table.
